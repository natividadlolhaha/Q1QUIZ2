Cars 2

Cars 2 is an animated movie about Lightning McQueen, a race car that is about to participate in the World Grand Prix. His friend, Mater, goes to a handful of countries as part of his spy missions. Mater later meets Finn McMissile, a British spy, as well as his colleague Holley Shiftwell. They soon discover that an evil plan has been created that threatens the race.
